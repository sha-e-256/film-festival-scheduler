<p class="has-medium-font-size"><strong>A nationally recognized, industry-leading cultural destination.</strong></p>

<h2 class="has-large-font-size" style="margin-bottom:45px">Our Mission</h2>



<p class="has-medium-font-size"><strong>WIFF is a cultural, charitable organization that celebrates the art of cinema by showcasing Canadian and international film and filmmakers. Delivering exceptional experiences through film, WIFF strengthens and enriches community while promoting the creative economy.</strong></p>

<h2 class="has-white-color has-text-color">Our Values</h2>



<div class="wp-container-6 wp-block-columns has-white-color has-text-color" style="margin-bottom:50px">
<div class="wp-container-3 wp-block-column">
<h3>Diversity of Programming</h3>



<p>Increasing the exposure of Canadian and international film to all audiences and offering programming which appeals to and is representative of people in our community.</p>
</div>



<div class="wp-container-4 wp-block-column">
<h3>Integrity</h3>



<p>Developing and nurturing respectful relationships with each other, our volunteers, audience, partners and the film industry.</p>
</div>



<div class="wp-container-5 wp-block-column">
<h3>Education</h3>



<p>Being a vehicle to increase knowledge of film and the film industry.</p>
</div>
</div>



<div class="wp-container-10 wp-block-columns has-white-color has-text-color">
<div class="wp-container-7 wp-block-column">
<h3>Quality Of Life</h3>



<p>Contributing to the quality of life for Windsor-Essex residents that comes from having a vibrant arts community</p>
</div>



<div class="wp-container-8 wp-block-column">
<h3>Sustainability </h3>



<p>Operating under the highest standards that will ensure the continuance of the festival for years to come</p>
</div>



<div class="wp-container-9 wp-block-column">
<h3>Urban arts community</h3>
